French toast recipe

Home

Description

The French toast is the perfect easy to go recipe to start your day without wasting time to fill your stomach

French toast is best served when hot you can either eat it that way or add honey or syrup if you're the sweet tooth!

Ingredients

  • 6 thick slices of bread
  • 2 large ggs
  • 2/3Milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground cinnamon (optional)
  • Salt (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Whisker milk eggs, vanilla, cinnamon and salt togeher in a shallow bowl
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides
  4. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side
  5. Bon appetit!
